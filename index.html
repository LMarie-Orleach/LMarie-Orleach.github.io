I am an evolutionary biologist broadly interested in the effects of reproduction on evolution. I primarily use lab experiments and theoretical work to study fundamental topics in quantitative and population genetics. I am also fortunate to collaborate with interesting people on literature reviews, field studies and meta-analyses.

My research interests include:

  • mating systems evolution
  • phenotypic plasticity
  • sexual conflicts
  • sexual selection
  • speciation

I currently work at the University of Rennes 1 (France) with Sylvain Glémin on a project funded by the ANR on the evolution of mating systems.
